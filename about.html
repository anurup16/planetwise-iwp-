About Planetwise

Our Goal

At Planetwise, our goal is to promote sustainable development education globally. We aim to provide high-quality educational resources that empower individuals and communities to make informed decisions about the environment, social equity, and economic development. We believe in a future where everyone plays a role in preserving our planet for future generations.

Where We Are Located

Planetwise is headquartered in Chennai, India. Our office is a hub of innovation, where a diverse team of educators, environmentalists, and technologists collaborate to try and create an impact to make this world a better place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate, inspire, and empower individuals across the globe to take actionable steps toward sustainable living. Through our portal, we offer courses, workshops, and resources designed to equip users with the knowledge and skills needed to address the pressing environmental challenges of our time.

Our Vision

We envision a world where education is the cornerstone of sustainable development, where every person has access to the knowledge they need to live sustainably, and where our collective efforts lead to a healthier planet.
